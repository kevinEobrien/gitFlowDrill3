Making French Toast

  • Procure bread, butter, vanilla, cinnamon, milk, and eggs.
  • In a bowl, mix one egg for each piece of toast you intend to make, then add a splash of vanilla and a dash of cinnamon. Beat thoroughly.
  • Heat the pan to medium hihg heat and melt the butter in it.
  • Dunk the bread in the egg mixture then put on the pan.
  • Cook each side for a few minutes until browned.
  • Add desired topings and enjoy!
